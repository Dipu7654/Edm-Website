About Us

Since 1984, EDM SERVICE has offered a full range of products and services for electroerosion machining , milling, polishing and bar turning.

More than 4000 customers in France and abroad trust and regularly use the products and services of the company EDM SERVICE.

With a stock of more than 10,500 references , a graphite machining workshop , and an after-sales service workshop , a sales department divided into 6 zones throughout France , the EDM SERVICE team is there to answer your questions.
